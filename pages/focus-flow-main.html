focusflow

FocusFlow helps you choose and prioritize subjects for your exam preparation. Here’s how you can use it:

  1. Select Your Current Subjects: Choose your current subjects from the list (you can add up to five main subjects).
  2. Manually Add Subjects: If your subject isn’t listed by default, you can manually add it.
  3. Start Prioritizing Your Subjects: Once selected, start prioritizing your subjects based on importance.
  4. Answer Questions to Determine Importance: Answer the provided questions to determine the relative importance of each subject.
  5. Receive a Summary: At the end, you’ll receive a summary highlighting the subjects you should focus on.
